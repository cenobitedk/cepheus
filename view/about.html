About DYNATRON

Interplanetary synth music.
Inspired by space and science-fiction, movies and soundtracks. In particular Jean-Michel Jarre, Alien, Blade Runner and 80's rock.

Timeline

  • 2017

    Dynatron is working on the third album while playing various shows in Europe with, among others artists, College (FR), Daniel Deluxe (DK) and Christine (FR).

  • 2016

    Dynatron signs with finnish label Blood Music. One of the biggest and most prolific labels in the genre, also harboring major artists Perturbator (FR), Gost (US) and Dan Terminus (FR).

    The first live shows are performed in October/November in France, Finland and Hungary.

    On November 4 the "The Rigel Axiom" EP is released on Blood Music.

  • 2015

    On September 4 the second album entitled "Aeternus" is released by Aphasia Records.

    The album is very well received and highly rated among fans and reviewers with words as "masterpiece", "meticulous" and "cinematic".

  • 2014

    On May 23 the movie "Cold in July" hit the theaters (USA) featuring "Cosmo Black" from the "Fireburner" EP.

    On September 25 the "Throttle Up" EP is released by Rad Rush Records.

  • 2013

    On May 5 the "Flashbacks" EP is released by Aphasia Records.

  • 2012

    DYNATRON has arrived.

    The first 4 tracks is published on Soundcloud during April and May.

    On September 8 the "Fireburner" EP is released.

    On November 22 the first album entitled "Escape Velocity" is relased by Aphasia Records.
