About DYNATRON

An Introduction

  • 2012

    DYNATRON is here. The first 4 tracks is published on Soundcloud during April and May. The first EP "Fireburner EP" is announced in August and released on September 8 by Aphasia Records.

    A few exclusive tracks and remixes is published before the first album entitled "Escape Velocity" is announced and relased on November 22, again by the fast growing Aphasia Records.

  • 2013

    On May 5 the "Flashbacks EP" is released.

  • 2014

    On September 25 the "Throttle Up EP" is released.

  • 2015

    The second album "Aeternus" is set for release in the second quarter of 2015.
